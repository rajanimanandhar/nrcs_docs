# Objectives
The NRCS Kaski District Chapter places its highest operational priority on blood service management and emergency transfusion support. The key objectives are:
1. Raise Awarness about voluntary blood donation.

→ To educate the public about the necessity of frequent blood donations and to foster a culure of humanitarianism within the community

2. Maintain a volunteer donor network

→ To create and expand volunteer blood donor database for use in emergency scenarios.

3. Organize blood donation programs 

→ Regular blood donation drives will be held in partnership with colleges, offices, local communities, youth groups, and organizations to maintain a steady supply of blood throughout the year.

4. Ensure Safe and Timely Blood availability

→ To maintain an efficient and dependable blood supply system so that patients in hospitals and during emergencies receive the appropriate blood type when needed.

5. Perform blood testing and cross-matching procedures 

→ To ensure safety, undertake laboratoy testing, screening, blood grouping, and cross-matching priot to transfusion in accordance with medical safety regulations.

6. Event and Emergency handling 

→ To guarantee the efficient handling of blood donation calls, events, donor coordination, and emergency response, including blood unit transportation and 24-hour service preparedness.
