# Introduction
The Nepal Red Cross Society, Kaski District Chapter (NRCS Kaski) is the district-level branch of the Nepal Red Cross Society (NRCS), a volunteer-based humanitarian organization that promotes relief, health and community service across Nepal. NRCS itself was founded in 1963 (2020 B.S) and officially recognized by the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) and affiliated with the Interational Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Socities (IFRC) in 1964.

NRCS Kaski has its base in Pokhara, Kaski district, Gandaki Province and serves a wide network of sub-chapters, junior and youth circles, volunteers, and community units collaborating with government, non-government organizations and social institutions. Its fundamental purpose is to provide essential services. including blood transfusion service, disaster relief, ambulance support, first aid, drinking water and sanitation (WASH), and metallic improved cooking stove, promoting health and disaster-preparedness, support community development and act impartially without discrimination. Through its Blood Transfusion Service Center, NRCS Kaski manages the complete lifecycle of blood service from organizing voluntary blood donation programs, to laboratory screening and cross matching, to distribution of blood units to patients and hospital. The chapter’s identity and public value are most strongly rooted in its commitment to safe blood service and life saving support during critical medical needs.


# Project Overview
The NRCS Kaski Blood Service Management System is a software solution designed to digitalize and automate the end-to-end workflow of blood donation and transfusion services.
The system manages:

1. Blood donation event creation and donor participation tracking
2. Donor registration, eligibility management, and donation history
3. Blood inventory recording, laboratory testing, and cross-matching
4. Blood issue and transfer to hospitals/patients with billing
5. Hospital accounts, transaction history, and service reporting
6. Report generation and analytics

The platform supports multiple user roles and ensure secure CRUD operations, proper audit trails, and accurate data traceability for every stage — from blood collection to transfusion. The system is expected to operate continuously, supporting both routine and emergency operations.
